Trial by Trivia

Step into the intellectual arena of Trial by Trivia, where knowledge reigns supreme. This innovative game blends the drama of a courtroom trial with the excitement of a general knowledge quiz. Participants must present their cases and defend their answers with eloquence and acumen. Only the sharpest minds will prevail in this thrilling battle of wit and wisdom.

Venue: PG Lab, Innovation Lab

1:00pm - 4:00pm

Flip Flop

Step into the whimsical realm of Flip Flop, where memory meets strategy in a dazzling display of code and cognition. Teams delve into the challenge of matching pairs among 20 cards adorned with intricate codes and outputs. With each successful match, points are earned, leading to an exhilarating race for victory. Engage your mind and unleash your skills in this captivating game of memory and wit.

Venue: Project Lab

1:00pm - 4:00pm

Fastest Finger First

Dive into the pulse-pounding 'Fastest Finger First' coding competition at Codefiesta. With lightning speed, conquer HTML, SQL, and DSA rounds in just 5 minutes each. Fill in the blanks with precision, turning code fragments into works of art. Harness your skills, embrace the challenge, and emerge triumphant in this epic test of coding prowess.

Venue: Hardware Lab
